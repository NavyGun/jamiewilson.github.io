Email Debt Forgiveness

Created by the hosts of the Reply All podcast, Email Debt Forgiveness Day is meant to encourage people to go ahead and resond to any neglected emails.

If there’s an email response you’ve wanted to send but been too anxious to send, you can send it . . . without any apologies or explanations for all the time that has lapsed. . . . Together, we can all make our inboxes less stressful.

— Alex Goldman, Reply All

But if you're like me, you need to send these types of emails much more often than one measley day a year. So, feel free to use this page all year round as your substitute for any apologies or explantions.
