Stylesheets

A community-generated collection of the best CSS resources, created to give the web design community a place to share and catalog all the best tools, tutorials, snippets, and repos.

Corpus

Corpus is a small CSS toolkit. It’s basically a collection of the things I find myself returning to for each new project. This includes a flexbox grid system, base typography, white space utility classes and a bit more.

Predawn

A dark interface and syntax theme for Sublime Text and Atom. It’s, like, super flat, pretty minimal and mostly awesome. And it made the Top 100 on Package Control!

Welderwire

The internal social-network of the WELD co-working community that helps our members stay connected outside the workspace and from across the country. Built with MeteorJs on top of Telescope.

Dewey

A Chrome app allows you to add tags, screenshots, and provides smarter search to your Chrome bookmarks.

Norwester

A free and open-source condensed, geometric, sans-serif display typeface with uppercase, small caps, numbers & an assortment of symbols.
